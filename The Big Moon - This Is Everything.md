Album / Artist: Here Is Everything / The Big Moon  

Label: Fiction	 

Release Date: October 14, 2022  
Review Date: 1/8/2023  
Reviewer: DJ Stace

General Comments / Reviews:  

Great guitar driven indie power-pop. - DJ Stace

A revealing, continually powerful set of songs, ‘Here Is Everything’ is a fantastically engaging experience, arguably the strongest set The Big Moon have placed their name against. -Clash Music

From their pen through to their sound, ‘Here Is Everything’ is emotive and glossy; one that gives space to breathe in this busy world. - NME


Release Notes:  

Here Is Everything is the third studio album by British indie rock band the Big Moon, released on 14 October 2022 via Fiction Records.[1] - Wikipedia


FCCs: None

RIYL: Sharon van Etten, Angel Olson, Caroline Rose, U.S. Girls

Tracklist:  

1. ***** 2 Lines 04:00 - Midtempo, driving guitar pop anthem. Perfect.
	
2. *** Wide Eyes 03:30 - Soaring diva anthem
	
	
3. Daydreaming 04:03 - Uptempo, pop. Maybe a bit saccharine. 
	
4. *** This Love 04:53 - Midtempo with twangy acoustic guitar riff, throbbing bass beat and deep throaty vocals. A little bit reminiscent of Nico. 
	
	
5. **** Sucker Punch 04:30 - C&W blues rock ballad. Strumming and jangly. Killer.
	
	
6. My Very Best 04:24 - Long build buzzing intro (14 seconds), builds to piano-driven midtempo Bruce Springsteen-esque rock anthem. 
	
	
7. ***** Ladye Bay 04:33 - Great uptempo rhythmic acoustic rocker. Expansive chorus. Another perfect pop rock track. Segue with PJ Harvey. 
	
	
	
8. **** Trouble 03:51 - Driving danceable beat. Anthemic chorus. Segue with Spoon. 
	
	
	
9. High & Low 03:35 - Slow, soaring piano ballad. 
	
	
10. **** Magic 04:02 - Krautrock disco rhythms, new wave lyrical sensibilities. glittery, uptempo dance track. 
	
	
11. **** Satellites 05:11 - Slow to midtempo piano strumming handclap ballad. Melodic and almost Shoegazey, almost.
