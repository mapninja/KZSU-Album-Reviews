Album / Artist: RESET / Panda Bear & Sonic Boom  

Label: Domino Recording Company	 

Release Date: August 12, 2022  
Review Date: 1/8/2023  
Reviewer: DJ Stace

General Comments / Reviews:  

A glorious playful ode to Brian Wilson. 

Release Notes:  

Reset is an album by Panda Bear and Sonic Boom (the respective stage names of Noah Lennox and Peter Kember). It marks the duo's first collaborative album; Kember had previously co-produced Lennox's Tomboy (2011) and Panda Bear Meets the Grim Reaper (2015). 

FCCs: None

RIYL: Panda Bear, Animal Collective, Brian Wilson & his Beach Boys

Play: All, Favs Rated with up to *****


Tracklist:  

1. ***** Gettin’ To The Point	 2:30 - Joyous, uptempo flamenco-guitar driven Beach Boys song, seemingly created by a generative AI. 
1. Go On	4:46 - Robotic, spaceship pop. Layers of harmony with 80's arcade battle soundbeds. 
1. *** Everyday	3:52 - Low-toned, repetitive, midtempo, snappy, strumming and buzzy, and somehow also light and uplifting. 
1. **** Edge Of The Edge	4:48 - Another heavenly 4-part harmony-layered midtempo Brian Wilson song that Brian Wilson never wrote.
1. *** In My Body - 3:51 - Slow, psych-tape-loop beauty. Bass and vocals are dominant. 
1. *** Whirlpool	5:01 - Midtempo droning trance dancer with light harmonic interludes. 
1. ***** Danger	5:38 - Another mid-tempo bright guitar-centric harmonic layer cake. 
1. **** Livin’ In The After	2:54 - 70's Movie Soundtrack Bossa Nova rhythms, more layers of harmony, castanets & strings. 
1. *** Everything’s Been Leading To This	 5:08 - Video game theme music intro gives way to everything but the kitchen sink... Jingle bells, harmonies, space invaders synths all crescendoing and then ending in a long fade.
