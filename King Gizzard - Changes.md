Album / Artist: Changes / King Gizzard And The Lizard Wizard
Label: KGLW	 

Release Date: October 28, 2022   
Review Date: 1/8/2023  
Reviewer: DJ Stace

General Comments / Reviews:  

More mushroom-space-prog from the current heavyweight champions of the genre. 


Release Notes:  

Changes is the 23rd studio album by Australian psychedelic rock band King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard, released on 28 October 2022. It is the third album the band released during October of 2022 and fifth and final album to be released overall during the year.

FCCs: 

RIYL: King Tuff, Brian Jonestown Massacre, psilocybin.

Play: All, Favs Rated with up to *****

Tracks:  

Tracklist:  

1. **** Change - 13:03 - Breathy, midtempo space-psych sprawling jam-out. Excellent.    
2. *** Hate Dancin’ - 3:16 - Killer late-70's Hall & Oates electric piano intro. Mid to Uptempo, with tempo shifts, along the way.   
3. *** Astroturf - 7:33 - Warped Shuggie Otis style anti-turf screed.  
4. No Body - 3:42 - Slow, loping syrupy Hotel California vibed meditation on dying.  
5. *** Gondii - 4:57 - 80's Cop Movie Theme Driving electro-beat rocker.  
6. *** Exploding Suns - 4:40 - Slow, high-hat driven, groovy. 
7. Short Change - 2:50 - Psych-prog jam outro based upon the first track.
